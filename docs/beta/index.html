Info

What is Games HEL?

Games HEL is a one-day mini-conference in Helsinki focusing solely on Console and PC games. We want it to be a space for game makers to share their knowledge and experience with their peers.

Where? When?

It's going to be a small, intimate event with ~100 attendees from the Finnish dev scene on Saturday, February 2nd.
We'll take over Games Factory for a day of talks, followed by evening mingling and finishing the night with an afterparty.
